# Quidey

Most students don't fail because they don't study.
They fail because they spend too much time figuring out *what* to study, *when*, and *for how long* — instead of actually doing it.

Quidey removes that overhead. You tell it what you're studying for and when it's due. It builds your plan, prioritizes your sessions, and tells you exactly what to do next.

No decisions. Just execution.

---

## How it works

1. Enter your subjects, their due dates, and how confident you are in each
2. Set how much time you have per day
3. Quidey generates a structured study plan, ordered by urgency
4. Follow it — session by session

The system weighs deadlines and confidence levels to decide what you should focus on first, and adjusts as you progress.

---

## Features

- **Urgency-based prioritization** — subjects are ranked dynamically based on due date and your self-assessed confidence
- **Pomodoro-style sessions** — study time is broken into focused blocks with optional breaks
- **Session planner** — a full day-by-day breakdown of what to study and when
- **Manual session mode** — start a session directly without generating a full plan
- **Multi-language support** — available in English and German
- **Color themes** — multiple visual themes including light, dark, mosaic, and default

---

## Usage

Quidey runs entirely in the browser — no installation required.

Visit **[quidey.com](https://quidey.com)** to get started.
